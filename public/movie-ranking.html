Things that I enjoyed this year by Devan!

My top three pieces of media from this year

Five nights at freddies

While the film seemed to be written mostly for children, it showed promise with the themes it presented. The potential of it becoming a series is extremely high.

Lilo and Stitch

A childhood classic makes all of the rougher times in life much easier to on your palette.

Master Detective Archives: Rain Code

While slightly cheating with this it is a visual novel game which is similar to a movie. It showcases the life of an amnesiac detective who has to rediscover his purpose, memories and protect his new friends from a looming threat.
